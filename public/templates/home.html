QUILT & BLUEGRASS FESTIVAL

November 21, 2015 10am - 5pm

What:

A one-day festival celebrating the art of quilting and the art of Bluegrass on the beautiful grounds of Thornebrook Village

Where:

Thornebrook Village, 2441 NW 43rd Street, Gainesville, FL

When:

Saturday, November 21, 2015 10 am – 5 pm
Quilt exhibit 10am – 4pm
Bluegrass from 10am - 5pm

Contact: Lyn White, Director lynwhite@bellsouth.net 352-682-2662
Thornebrook Village, the Quilters of Alachua County Day Guild and the Tree City Quilters’ Guild have put together a wonderfully delightful day for all to enjoy the bright colors and designs of quilting with Bluegrass music playing in the background. Stroll through the tree-shaded beauty of Thornebrook Village looking at the quilts, grab a bite to eat at the restaurants, gaze in the shops, and stop and listen to great local music in the stage area. Quilts from the many quilters of Alachua County and surrounding counties will be shown with some for sale. The local quilt guilds will provide “white glove” services for those wishing to take a closer look at a quilt to better understand the types and techniques involved in making a quilt and what constitutes a quilt. Local Bluegrass bands gathered by Bill Hutchinson will provide shoe-tapping enjoyment: 10am - Wayfarin’ Strangers, 11am – Patchwork, 12 noon - Wayfarin’ Strangers, 1pm – Patchwork, 2pm - Wayfarin’ Strangers, 3pm - Boulware Family Band, 4pm - Accidental Cowboys, 5pm - Boulware Family Band. Come and join us for a day of fun, art and entertainment!
